geometrical_derivatives

By Pierre Beaujean (pierre.beaujean@unamur.be).

Version 0.2 (Development).

Synopsis

geometrical_derivatives - Fetch the different derivatives of the energy with respect to geometrical modifications

usage: geometrical_derivatives [-h] [-v] [-N] [infile]

Positional arguments:

infile

source of the derivatives

Optional arguments:

-h, --help

show this help message and exit

-v, --version

show program’s version number and exit

-N, --no-vibs

Do not perform the vibrational analysis

More information

Try to fetch the gradient and hessian. If the second one is found, compute vibrational frequencies. Rely on the availability of geometrical_derivatives.
